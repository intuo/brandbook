Data visualization

Presenting data visually to uncover surprising patterns and observations that wouldn't be apparent from looking at stats alone.

Contents

  • Chart
  • Statistics
  • Filter

Chart

Areacharts and linecharts are used to visualize the growth of data over time.

The graph library of choice is Highcharts. As mentioned in the frontend guidelines we use a Javascript specific selector js-graph to initialze Highchart on the element.

Skills over time

  <div class="js-graph" style="height: 350px;"></div>

Statistics

Statistic cards are used to show the current and past total of a specific data set. They always have current value, and a comparison of growth over time.

The color of the statistics can be altered by altering the modifier of stat-value to e.g. is-warning. Additional color codes can be found in frontend guidelines.

Filter

Filters are mandatory for charts, but optional for tables. Filtering should be solely limited to This week, This month and All time.

The active state of a panel-filter is is-active.
This week This month All time 
  <button class="panel-filter is-active">This week</button>
  <button class="panel-filter">This month</button>
  <button class="panel-filter">All time</button>
