Search box

The search box is used to search in large amounts of data in tables, or different kinds of lists.

    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-xs-12">
        <div class="filter-left">
          <div class="input-group">
            <span class="input-group-addon">
              <span class="ion-search"></span>
            </span>
            <input type="text" class="form-control" placeholder="Search">
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

Multiple filters

A search box can be combined with a dropdown menu. UI-wise you should opt for a maximum of 4 filters per page. 

  <div class="filter">
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-xs-6">
        <div class="filter-left">
          <div class="input-group">
            <span class="input-group-addon">
              <span class="ion-search"></span>
            </span>
            <input type="text" class="form-control" placeholder="Search">
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="col-xs-6">
        <div class="filter-right">
          <div class="filter-select">
            <select data-placeholder="Sort by" class="chosen-select" tabindex="-1">
              <option selected>All certificates</option>
              <option>Product expert</option>
              <option>New marketing hire</option>
              <option>New accounting hire</option>
              <option>Sales onboarding</option>
              <option>Senior</option>
            </select>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
