What do we do?

We offer a talent management solution that combines technology and research and facilitates coaching to manage and improve employee engagement, knowledge, and performance.

Talent management solution

Talent management has a broad spectrum, we aim at solving the leading factors: Engagement, performance, and growth.

Technology

We offer a hybrid solution that combines technology and human interaction.

Research

We embrace qualitative and quantitative research to improve our service offerings.

Facilitate coaching

We help individual or grouped coaches in our partner-network in their day to day activities. We can do this by giving them valuable information to enhance the quality of their service offerings. Secondly, we help them to scale their own business because we automate or enhance the time-consuming tasks that are not delivering immediate value to their clients. This means that we create a portal where coaches can manage their and our clients. We help them with sharing knowledge, growing their business and creating long-term relationships with their customers.

Employee engagement

The INTUO platform delivers and captures real-time insights and tips to team leaders to enhance the overall employee engagement.

Knowledege

Our solution offers a modern way to capture, measure and distribute knowledge throughout the organization.

Employee performance

We offer various ways to manage and align professional and personal goals. Our tools and dedicated coaching ignite the development of a growth mindset and enhance business performance and employability.
